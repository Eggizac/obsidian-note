# In english
Healthy eating is one of the most important things we can do for our health. Our body is like a machine. It needs good fuel to work properly. When we eat the right foods, we help our heart, brain, and muscles stay strong. Eating healthy food does not only make us look better, it also makes us feel better inside.

Food gives our body energy. We need energy to walk, study, play, and even sleep. The food we eat changes into energy inside our body. Healthy food helps this process work well. If we eat too much unhealthy food, our body can become tired and slow. That is why a balanced diet is very important.

A balanced diet means eating different kinds of food every day. There are five main food groups that our body needs: fruits, vegetables, grains, protein foods, and dairy products. Each group gives us something special.

Fruits and vegetables are full of vitamins and minerals. They help our body fight illness and keep our skin healthy. Scientists say that people should eat at least five portions of fruit and vegetables every day. A portion can be one apple, one banana, or a small bowl of salad.

Grains like bread, rice, and pasta give us carbohydrates. Carbohydrates are the main source of energy for our body. Whole-grain foods, such as brown rice or whole wheat bread, are healthier than white bread or sugary cereals because they have more fiber. Fiber helps our stomach work well and keeps us full longer.

Protein foods help our body grow and repair cells. Protein comes from meat, chicken, fish, eggs, beans, and nuts. People who do not eat meat can get protein from plant foods like lentils or tofu. Scientists say that protein is especially important for children and teenagers because they are still growing.

Dairy products give us calcium, which helps keep our bones and teeth strong. Milk, cheese, and yogurt are good sources of calcium. Some people drink plant milk made from soy, almonds, or oats. Many of these drinks also contain calcium.

Water is another part of healthy eating. Our body is made mostly of water, and it needs water to work well. Drinking enough water helps us stay cool, think clearly, and clean our body from inside. Most experts recommend drinking six to eight glasses of water every day.

We also need to be careful with foods that have too much sugar, salt, or fat. These foods can be tasty, but they are not healthy if we eat them too often. Fast food, sweets, and soft drinks can make us feel tired and can cause health problems later. It is better to enjoy them only sometimes.

Healthy eating is not about being perfect. It is about making good choices most of the time. We can start with small steps: eating more fruit, drinking more water, and choosing fresh food instead of processed snacks. Over time, our body will feel stronger, and our mind will feel clearer. Good food is good life.
