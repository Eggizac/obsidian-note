This happens because many people don’t realize that their diet is very low in **fiber, protein, and other essential nutrients**, while containing too many **carbohydrates, sugar, and other harmful substances**.  
As a result, they often feel tired and unwell.

To feel better, people should:

1. Eat foods that are **high in protein and fiber**.
2. Avoid foods that contain **a lot of sugar and other unhealthy ingredients**.
3. **Consume a sufficient amount of carbohydrates** — but choose healthy sources like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
